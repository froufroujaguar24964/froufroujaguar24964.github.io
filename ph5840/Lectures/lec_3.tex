\(Tr(\hat{\rho} _{A}^{2})\) is called purity. \\
Measure of metric for entanglement \(1 - Tr(\hat{\rho} _{A}^{2})\).\\ 
\(1 - Tr(\hat{\rho} _{A}^{2})\) is \(\frac{1}{2}\) for Mixed states and 0 for separable states.
